    1. AI Pets Exist - Real pets, while charming, have a few drawbacks, according to pet-bot developers. Real pets need to be cared for, including feeding and cleaning.     2. Most AI Bots are Female - According to studies, the majority of people prefer the sound of a female voice over that of a male voice. It's because when you ask Alexa or Siri a question, you'll get a lovely and courteous woman's voice as an answer.

    3. AI Recognises Emotions - Kismet, a robot created in the late 1990s, can recognize emotions in human body language and voice tone.
